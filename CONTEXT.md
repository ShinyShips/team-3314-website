# CONTEXT

Glossary of terms for the Team 3314 website. The site speaks to two audiences:
**outward** (sponsors, press, prospective members — most pages) and **inward**
(current students and their parents — the Team Info page).

## Team Info page

The inward-facing one-stop-shop page (`TEAM INFO` in the nav) where current
students and parents find everything they need: upcoming dates, team documents,
meeting logistics, and how to get plugged into team communications. Distinct
from **About** (who we are, outward) and **Join** (prospective members).

## Team Calendar

The single Google Calendar, owned by the team's Workspace, that holds all
dates relevant to students and parents — meetings, competitions, deadlines,
travel. It is the source of truth for "when is the next thing"; the Team Info
page displays it but never stores dates itself.

## Team Documents folder

The single publicly-viewable Google Drive folder, owned by the team's
Workspace, that holds every document the Team Info page shows. Whatever is in
the folder is on the site — uploading a file there *is* publishing it. Nothing
containing personal data (rosters with contact details, medical forms,
addresses) may be placed in it.

## Document section

A grouping of documents on the Team Info page, defined entirely by a subfolder
of the Team Documents folder (e.g. "Forms", "Parent Info"). Creating a
subfolder creates a section; loose files in the folder root appear in a
general section. Sections are one level deep.

## Quick-info block

The static at-a-glance card on the Team Info page: regular meeting days/times,
location, contacts, and communication channels. Edited in code, unlike the
calendar and documents which update live from Google.
